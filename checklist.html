Which of these have you seen in your classroom?

Check every sign of disengagement you have personally noticed. Be honest — there are no wrong answers here.

Obvious Signs Easy to spot
✓
Phone out under the desk or head down on the table
✓
Stopped raising their hand, stopped contributing to discussions, stopped volunteering for anything
✓
Grades dropping in a way that does not match what you know they are capable of
✓
Making excuses to avoid work, procrastinating, or not turning assignments in at all
✓
Saying it out loud — "this is boring," "why do we even need this"
Hidden Signs Easy to miss
✓
Shows up every day, has materials out, follows directions, but stares blankly at the board without writing anything down
✓
Appears to be listening but cannot answer a single question when called on
✓
Completes assignments but only does the bare minimum with no real thought or effort
✓
Fidgeting, tapping, or looking around the room while the lesson is happening
✓
Has not said a word about struggling or being frustrated — they just quietly stopped caring
